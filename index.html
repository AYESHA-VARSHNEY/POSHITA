Our Story

Rooted in tradition,
refined for today.

POSHITA began with a simple idea: dry fruits should taste the way they did at your grandmother's house — rich, fresh, and honest. We work directly with orchards across the country, choosing quality over shortcuts at every step.

Every batch is cleaned, sorted, and sealed in small quantities so what reaches you is always fresh, never sitting in a warehouse for months.

Direct-from-farm sourcing, no middlemen
Small-batch cleaning and grading
Sealed fresh, shipped fast
